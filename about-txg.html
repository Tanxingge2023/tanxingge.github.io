About TanXG

TanXG was founded in 2023 by D.Liang and his friends right after the National College Entrance Examination. The name "TanXG" comes from our high school class, symbolizing that knowledge and truth are like stars in the sky, and we are the stargazers who keep exploring and pursuing them.

Just like the traditional studios of ancient scholars, TanXG serves as a modern club for students and researchers — a vibrant platform for those who crave knowledge and pursue truth.

"Seeking Truth" and "Pursuing Goodness" are not only our high school class motto but also the core mission of TanXG.

Seeking Truth means we are committed to exploring and sharing genuine knowledge. Most of our content is original, and we clearly indicate the source for all reprinted materials.

Pursuing Goodness means all projects of TanXG are non-profit. Our goal is to provide a free platform for students and scholars who love science and exploration, and to contribute to the scientific culture of our society.

It should be noted that TanXG is currently run by university students with limited experience. Therefore, all resources and materials on this site are for reference only, and we welcome corrections and suggestions.

We sincerely welcome you to join us and explore the stars of knowledge together! Contact: tanxg2023@163.com
